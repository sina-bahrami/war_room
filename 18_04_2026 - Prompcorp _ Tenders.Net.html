Details: PSC002756. Nuclear Medicine Manufacturing Program - Project management services.
ANSTO is seeking an experienced PMS provider to support the delivery of the Nuclear Medicine Manufacturing Program (NMMP) at its Lucas Heights campus, NSW.
The PMS provider will deliver embedded project and program leadership and operate as an integral member of ANSTO’s NMMP Delivery Team. The role spans the full lifecycle of the Program, including planning, design, procurement, construction, commissioning, and transition to operations. The PMS provider will be responsible for integrated program co-ordination and management, providing proactive, evidence-based advice to ANSTO, and supporting informed decision-making across scope, schedule, cost, risk, and quality. The role requires active engagement with internal ANSTO stakeholders, regulators, delivery partners, consultants, and subject matter experts to manage interfaces and ensure alignment across all workstreams.

Details: DECYP 2386. Healthy School Lunch Program.
The Healthy School Lunch Program is a significant Government initiative aimed at addressing food insecurity and supporting student wellbeing. Moving from delivering around 3,000 meals per week to 15 schools in 2022, it has now expanded to deliver over 21,000 meals per week to 60 schools from 2026.
Ongoing delivery of the program beyond 2026 will aim to support families with the increasing cost-of-living pressures. In addition to this, it will enable children from low socio-economic backgrounds to be well-nourished and ready to learn and continue to contributing to improved attendance and engagement outcomes.

Details: 260402. Catering services Panel Request for Proposal (RFP).
Edith Cowan University (ECU) is seeking responses to this Request for Proposal (RFP) from suitable, experience and professional Catering services.
The objective of this Request for Proposal is to establish a multi-supplier Catering services panel that provides the University with access to capable, compliant, and value for money catering services. The panel will support events of varying size, complexity, and profile, including (but not limited to) high value and high profile University functions.
Edith Cowan University (ECU) hosts a diverse range of events throughout the year, including academic functions, corporate engagements, student activities, cultural events, and high-profile stakeholder occasions. Catering services play a critical role in supporting these events and in upholding the University’s reputation for quality, professionalism, and hospitality.
To meet ongoing and future catering requirements, the University is seeking to establish a panel of suitably qualified and experienced Catering Service Providers. This panel will enable the University to efficiently engage reliable suppliers who can deliver consistent, high-quality catering services across a variety of event types, sizes, and formats.
This Request for Proposal (RFP) invites submissions from providers who demonstrate the capability, capacity, and commitment to meet the University’s standards, compliance requirements, and operational needs.  
The vendors will be required to:
- Support events of varying size, complexity, and profile, including high value and high-profile University functions.
- Enable access to a range of catering styles and service models, from simple refreshments to full-service catering.
- Ensure compliance with relevant University policies, food safety regulations, workplace health and safety requirements, and sustainability expectations.
Requirements: 
- Provision of light refreshments, including tea, coffee, and morning and afternoon tea
- Corporate and business lunch catering, including boxed lunches and buffet style offerings
- Catering for formal functions and special events
- Accommodation of dietary, allergen, and cultural requirements (including, but not limited to, vegetarian, vegan, halal, and gluten-free options)
- Compliance with food safety standards and the delivery of consistent quality and reliable service
- Delivery, setup, and pack down of catering services as required
- Provision of sustainable packaging solutions and food waste minimisation initiatives (desirable).

Common Issues + FAQs

What does 'Availability Unknown' Mean?

Tenders.Net does not know of any electronic documentation or electronic tender boxes for this opportunity at this time. Please refer the tender contact details instead.

If any such information is later released, we will send out the advertisement again with the [Updated] flag.


I Get 'Page Not Found' when I click on a link.

Please make sure you have an active connection to the internet and have not used up your data limit. If your connection is fine then the linked site may be temporarily down. We mark any link that goes outside of Tenders.Net with an asterisk ( * ).

If the link continues to fail you should then use the tender contact details instead for assistance.


I can't log in to the 'Documentation' or 'Lodgement' links.

If you are being taken to an external site - the link will be marked with an asterisk ( * ) - then your Tenders.Net login details will not work. You will need another access to that site.

If you are asked to connect to www.tenders.net and do not have an access please contact us or register to get a login. If you cannot remember your access you can retrieve your password here.


What is a 'Selective' or 'Invitation Only' Tender?

Unlike public tendering where anyone can make a response, some opportunities are restricted only to those who have been directly invited. These are sometimes the result of a public EOI.

You will not normally be able to respond to these opportunities unless you have been invited.
